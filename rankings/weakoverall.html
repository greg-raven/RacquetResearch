Rankings: Racquets for weak players

The following overall rankings are based on the official database of racquet measurements done by technicians at the U.S. Racquet Stringers Association, published in their magazine.

The rankings under the macro criteria were weighted thus: 1.5 * Elbow Safety + Wrist Safety + Shoulder Safety + Efficiency + 0.5×Dexterity. The difference between these rankings and those for expert players is that the macrocriterion of Dexterity is included here.
